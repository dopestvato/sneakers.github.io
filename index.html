Nike Set to Drop Air Max 90 Ultra 2.0 Flyknit in Classic Black and White

It’s not long until Air Max Day 2017 finally arrives and Nike hasn’t wasted any time detailing its biggest releases to celebrate the occasion. It’s also been revealed that the Nike Air Max 90 Ultra 2.0 Flyknit silhouette will soon drop in a timeless black and white colorway iteration. A modern take on the iconic runner, the uppers feature a detailed Flyknit construction, which is both lightweight and sturdy due to the hyperfuse overlay. The new drop leads up to the heavily-hyped Air Max Day Collection set to release on March 26, while this new model of the Nike Air Max 90 Ultra 2.0 Flyknit is scheduled for a March 2 release. In other news, infamous sneaker reseller Allen Kuo resurfaced online to flaunt his extensive YEEZY BOOST 350 V2 “Zebra” supply.

UNION
Teams Up With Vans On Three California-Inspired Colorways of the Old Skool

This week Union Los Angeles announced its first collaboration with fellow California brand Vans. It is actually strange that it took the two this long, considering that the store has been carrying Vans for many years. Good things take a while though, and the result is a beautiful pack of Vans Old Skools. The iconic silhouette comes in Dusty Pink, Muted Gold and Off White. Overall a simple design, yet with some striking details. A lux twill weave has been used across the upper of the sneaker, matching the buttery suede and the soft pebble grain leather used on the Vans stripe logo. Each shoe is tonal, down to the outer sole and the stitching. The UNION logo has been embossed on the tongue. The only contrasting detail on the sneaker are the top white eyelets. UNION will launch its new Vans collaboration with some accompanying apparel in the coming weeks. Stay tuned!
